Devlog #2.5 (17/11/2021)

The Project Vanguard website is live! This new site will serve as the hub for all things Project Vanguard, where you find everything from detailed descriptions of gameplay features, to fanart and news updates.

In order to kick off the new website, we will now be holding seomthing called Question Of The Week, where the dev team will take one of the community's questions once per week, and answer it in a detailed breakdown. We will also be holding fanart spotlights, where art made by the community will have a speciel spotlight section on either the Devlogs, QOTW posts, or both!
