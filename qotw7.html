Question Of The Week #7 - JAN 8, 2022
Will Vanguard be coming to consoles? What will servers be like? What about open-source??

Apologies to any rangers on console, but there are no plans to bring Vanguard to platforms outside of PC. As many of the community will also already know, we are also planning to have server-hosting and community servers be a big part of the game. A lot of the server architecture is still not set in stone, so we can’t go too deep into that just yet.

Many of you guys have also inquired about the degree to which Vanguard will be open-source. Will there just be custom skins, or can we unleash custom weapons upon all of matchmaking-dom? Sadly, the answer is still that it is too early to tell. If custom weapons, rangers, and any assortment of major gameplay features will be available to put into the game, they will be regulated to servers which allow those features.
