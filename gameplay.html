MECHS

PREPARE FOR DROP

A Mech is a massive advanced weapons platform, which in military scenarios is piloted and operated by a Ranger. The mechs of the interstellar era are common features of planetside battlefields, which are often deployed as walking tanks. Combined with the integration of the most cutting-edge technology, such as custodian-class AI, mechs have made tanks and other heavy vehicles almost irrelevant.

RANGERS

SPEED IS LIFE

A Ranger is a highly elite soldier who possesses highly sophisticated skills, equipment, and training compared to standard marines. Combined with various augmentations and physical enhancements, rangers are considered the most elite and fearsome soldiers of any battlefield. Due to their advanced skillsets and training, rangers are also the operators of mechs.

ARSENAL

LOCK AND LOAD

Rangers are armed with the most sophisticated and advanced weaponry and equipment humanity has to offer. As previously mentioned, rangers possess a jump-kit - an array of stabilization jets, thrusters, and miniaturized rocket technology. The jump-kit allows rangers to double and triple jump, leap further and higher, run on walls, slide for extended distances, hover in midair, dash from side to side, and so on. The jump-kit is also assisted by a lightweight exoskeleton worn on the legs, and is designed to increase jumping power and compensate for high levels of shock from landing at such great heights and high speeds. In addition, rangers also have undergone a variety of augmentation procedures to enhance their reaction speed, senses, and strength.

Rangers possess the best, most in-depth training of any infantryman fielded in the interstellar era. They are taught the history of combat, the most optimal strategies for every possible scenario, the intricacies of each piece of equipment, the entire history and commonly employed strategies of their enemies, and much more. As of the introduction of class-2, rangers are also trained in highly-specialized certifications, giving them high levels of expertise in their roles on the battlefield.

Combat training and exercises are performed within active virtual reality simulations. These simulations are performed by attaching the user to an intricate harness which is connected to an array of robotic arms and instruments. Then user can run, jump, and maneuver around the simulation as if in the real world.
